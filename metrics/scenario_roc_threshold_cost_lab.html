How the best point is chosen

Each threshold gives one ROC point. A cost isometric has slope \[ \frac{P(+)C_{FN}}{P(-)C_{FP}}. \] Slide that line toward the upper-left. The last attainable contact point is the threshold with minimum expected cost. At a smooth interior optimum, the ROC tangent and the isometric have the same slope.
