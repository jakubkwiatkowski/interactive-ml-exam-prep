Other Curves Lab

Interactive ROC, PR, DET, Lift, and Cost curves, with a movable threshold.
What the curves mean
- ROC: TPR vs FPR
- PR: Precision vs Recall
- DET: FNR vs FPR
- Lift: how much better the ranking is than random when taking the top fraction of ranked examples
- Cost curve: expected cost as operating conditions change
