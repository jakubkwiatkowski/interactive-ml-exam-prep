Arithmetic, Geometric and Harmonic Means Explorer

Compare the three means and see why F1 uses the harmonic mean while G-mean uses the geometric mean.
For positive values: \[ H(A,B)\le G(A,B)\le A_{arith}(A,B). \] All three are equal only when \(A=B\).

Equations

\[ Arithmetic=\frac{A+B}{2} \] \[ Geometric=\sqrt{AB} \] \[ Harmonic=\frac{2}{1/A+1/B}=\frac{2AB}{A+B} \]

The harmonic mean stays closest to the smaller value. The geometric mean also penalizes imbalance, but less aggressively. The arithmetic mean treats the two values additively.
